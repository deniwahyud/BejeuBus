History of BEJEU

Bongkotan Jati Utama or more commonly called BEJEU is a well-known bus fleet that serves trips inter-city and inter-province. This bus fleet was founded by a teak businessman, named H. Rofi'udin S.E. BEJEU began operating or opened AKAP route in 2007. And the following main routes have been served by BEJEU bus to date :

  • Jakarta - Jepara
  • Cibinong - Jepara
  • Merak - Cilegon - Serang - Jepara
